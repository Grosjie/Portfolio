Strengths

I am a Self Learner
This is a strength because independent learning is a trait that helps one throughout their life. Also, self-learning is more effective than taking help from others. It is good to ask for guidance and work in little groups but a student should be able to do a task by themself.

I am Organized
Organization is an important academic strength as it is good to do everything on a predetermined time.

I am curious
I will try to learn more than what is being told in the course books and avoid cramming the concepts. This allows me to learn independently through different sources like books or the internet.




Weakness

I do lack focus at times
Sometimes I struggle to keep focused and find it hard to concentrate during a lecture nor can I study for long hours.

I will Procrastinate
Sometimes I tend to put off work until the last moment and waste my time on other things.
